## 2. Persona definition

A persona is a fictional person that represents a group of people.

Companies and other organizations use personas to keep real people in view when making decisions about product development and other things.

## 3. Stereotype definition and example

In this task, you will look at stereotypical personas.

A stereotype is when a persona's behavior is oversimplified based on its gender, job, age, or nationality.

For example:

Non-stereotypical:
She evaluates AI tools pragmatically and adopts those that reduce her administrative workload.

Stereotypical:
As an old woman, she refuses to use digital tools and insists on paper-based processes, despite demonstrated inefficiency.

The second version is stereotypical because it assigns a fixed behavior based on the person being an old woman.
